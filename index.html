HERE'S OUR CULTURAL PROGRAMS!

HRPEC's cultural programs are also a way to raise awareness of human rights issues and promote a culture of respect for human rights. When people from different cultures come together to celebrate their shared humanity, it can help to break down barriers and promote understanding. This can create a more just and equitable society for everyone.

MIYERKULET!

is a regular Wednesday event
that provides
a safe space for the arts
and human rights advocacy.

SINE SYUDAD

is a community-based
film screening event
held in collaboration
with local stakeholders,
organizations, and institutions.

HRPEC CHIKAHAN

is a monthly roundtable discussion
that brings together experts
to address critical human rights issues
and develop actionable recommendations.
