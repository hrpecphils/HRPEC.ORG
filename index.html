The government must stop its opportunistic attempts to take over ancestral lands from IPs and farmers, and instead provide them with the aid they need to thrive.

The ICC's effort is a step forward from the years of deliberate inaction and silencing by the Duterte administration At best, it would offer reprieve for the victims of a senseless bloodbath-those who deserve justice the most.

STOP ILLEGAL MINING!

For decades, illegal mining has inflicted widespread planetary damage, posing heightened safety risks and fostering reckless behavior with lasting negative consequences. Human awareness and intervention are essential to prevent further destruction.
STOP ILLEGAL MINING!

STOP THE KILLINGS!
STOP POLICE BRUTALITY!

Since 2016, over 2,000 individuals in the Philippines have been extrajudicially killed by police in the government's "war on drugs," with limited officer accountability. This pervasive, systematic human rights issue demands investigation and intervention.

HERE'S OUR CULTURAL PROGRAMS!

HRPEC's cultural programs are also a way to raise awareness of human rights issues and promote a culture of respect for human rights. When people from different cultures come together to celebrate their shared humanity, it can help to break down barriers and promote understanding. This can create a more just and equitable society for everyone.
