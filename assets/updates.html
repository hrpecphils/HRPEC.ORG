MiyerKULET!: Kulay at Malay is off to a great start!

MiyerKULET! Kulay at Malay is experiencing a promising and successful beginning! Participants engaged in a variety of activities including watching films, engaging in conversations, cooking, singing, and performing poetry. The first five events of Kulay at Malay brought together a diverse group of human rights advocates who champion various causes and beliefs, along with like-minded individuals who share their values.

"Kulay at Malay" a series of events that focuses on promoting cultural diversity, human rights awareness, and creative expression. The events provided a platform for participants to come together and actively participate in a range of activities that fostered connection, understanding, and a shared commitment to human rights advocacy.

The diverse activities mentioned, such as watching movies, engaging in discussions, community cooking, singing, and reciting poetry, suggest a well-rounded approach to promoting cultural exchange and human rights awareness. These activities likely encouraged participants to engage with one another in meaningful ways, allowing them to learn from each other's perspectives and experiences.

This inclusivity and diversity highlight the importance of collaboration among people who may approach human rights issues from different angles but share a common goal of promoting justice, equality, and dignity for all.

By emphasizing that these activities were carried out "for the first five Kulay at Malay events," the statement suggests that this initiative has a growing and sustainable nature. The successful execution of the initial events likely bodes well for the future of Kulay at Malay, indicating that it has managed to attract and engage participants who are enthusiastic about its mission and activities.

MiyerKULET!: KULAY AT MALAY

Para sa Agosto at Setyembre, inihahandog ng HRPEC ang MiyerKULET! Kulay at Malay—isang selebrasyon ng ating mga wika, ng ating mga katutubo, ng ating magkakaibang pagkakakilanlan. Kasabay nito, ating aalalahanin ang Martial Law at ang rehimen ni Marcos Sr. Sa susunod na dalawang buwan, magpapalabas ang #MiyerKULET! ng 37 films nang may kasabay na talkback/diskusyon kasama ang iba’t ibang eksperto’t panauhin. Magkakaro’n din ng musical performances at cultural presentations!
Takits sa rooftop ng Fil Garcia Tower, Kalayaan Ave., QC!
